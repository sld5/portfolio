EVERY MAJOR'S TERRIBLE

DIRECTOR
CINEMATOGRAPHER
EDITOR

Every Major's Terrible is a humorous musical reenactment of a comic strip of the same name from the popular web comic xkcd. It was created as a fun promotional video for the Simon Fraser University Choir, whose members sang and acted in the video.

Making Every Major was a crazy but awesome experience, requiring many brief shoots at a multitude of different locations, filming shenanigans that included dipping fruit in liquid nitrogen, holding a pair of large snakes, running from an angry mob, and underwater basket weaving.

behind the scenes

One tricky aspect of this shoot was coordinating many choir members for certains shots who were not actors, however, they were great to work with and were very understanding of the filmmaking process. An ensemble of choir members also provided the vocals for the chorus of the song.

The underwater shots used a special waterproof camera (and a cameraman who can swim, unlike myself). The cameraman had to wrap his legs around a weight in order to stay submerged long enough to get the shot.

The exploding fruit scene used liquid nitrogen to freeze bananas. A fun practical effect!

All of the writing on the chalkboard for the math scene was legitimate equations from a high level math class, and some Latin. Like many shots for this video, there was quite a lot of prep time for what only needed to be a three second shot! But the attention to detail was definitely worth it.
